Nashville is the capital city of Tennessee located in the center within Davidson county. It is an urban city that was incorporated in 1806. The median household income for Nashville is 71,328 which is higher than the states median income of 64,035. The total population of Nashville is 689,447. The city is home to Vanderbilt University, one of the most prestigious schools in the United States.

Memphis is an urban city in Tennessee. It is located to the southwest within Shelby county. It is home to Rhodes College which is a renowned institution. Memphis has a population of 633,104. It has a median household income of 48,090; significantly lower than the states median household income of 64,035. The city was incorporated in 1826.
